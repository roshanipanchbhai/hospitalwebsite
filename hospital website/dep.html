Departments:

Neurology

Neurology is a branch of medicine that focuses on the study, diagnosis, and treatment of disorders and diseases of the nervous system. The nervous system is a complex network of cells and fibers that transmit signals between different parts of the body, allowing for communication and coordination of bodily functions.Details

Anesthesia

Anesthesia is a medical practice that involves the administration of medications to induce a temporary loss of sensation or awareness, primarily for the purpose of performing medical procedures or surgeries. It ensures that patients are comfortable, pain-free, and unaware during the procedure. Details

Dermatology

Dermatology is the branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of conditions related to the skin, hair, nails, and mucous membranes. Dermatologists are medical doctors who specialize in the field of dermatology and are trained to diagnose and manage a wide range of skin conditions. Details

Genetics

Genetics is the branch of biology that deals with the study of genes, heredity, and variation in living organisms. It encompasses the principles and mechanisms by which traits are passed from parents to offspring and how these traits can change over generations. Details

Cardiology

Cardiology is the branch of medicine that focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases and disorders related to the cardiovascular system, which includes the heart and blood vessels. Cardiologists are medical doctors who specialize in this field and are trained to manage various cardiovascular conditions. Details

Gynecology

Gynecology is a medical specialty that focuses on the health and well-being of the female reproductive system, including the uterus, ovaries, fallopian tubes, cervix, and vagina. Gynecologists are doctors who specialize in providing medical care and treatment for women's reproductive health issues. Details

Pathology

Pathology is the branch of medical science that involves the study of diseases and their effects on the structure and function of the body. It focuses on understanding the nature, causes, and mechanisms of diseases through laboratory examination of tissues, cells, and body fluids. Details

Radiology

Radiology is a branch of medicine that involves the use of medical imaging techniques to diagnose and treat various diseases and conditions. Radiologists are physicians who specialize in interpreting medical images and using them to provide diagnoses and recommendations for patient care. Details

Surgery

The Department of Surgery is a specialized branch within a healthcare institution, typically found in hospitals or medical centers. It is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of various surgical conditions and diseases. Surgeons are medical professionals who specialize in performing surgical procedures to address injuries, diseases, and deformities. Details

Pediatric

Pediatrics is a branch of medicine that focuses on the health and medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians are medical doctors who specialize in treating and managing the physical, behavioral, and mental health of children from birth through adolescence. Details
